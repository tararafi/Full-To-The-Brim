Post Secondary Admission Anxiety

Whether you are a Grade 12 student finished with applying to universities/colleges, or you are in Grade 9 simply worried about where you’ll end up, read below for some tips to combat the stress that comes with post secondary admission.

  • Be open to more than one college/university.

    Have several options and consider having back-up choices as well, in case you do not get into your top choices.

  • Don’t make post secondary applications and admissions a constant topic of conversation.

    This will only stress you out more, and not give yourself the time you need to take a break.

  • Stay connected with family and friends.

    Be sure to keep yourself surrounded with a strong support system of people who care about you and your wellbeing.
