Post Secondary Students

Post secondary students are adapting to a new form of learning unfathomable to learners of previous generations.

Along with many of the challenges that already come with adjusting to a different lifestyle as a post secondary student, individuals are now faced with a plethora of added struggles due to distanced learning.

Learn about ways you can manage online school, learn some healthy studying habits and gain some insight into job searching for post graduation!
